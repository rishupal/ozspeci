Oz Species Adventure

This is a survival game. You would have 100 food point at the beginning.

Picking up fruit(10 points) and soda(20 points) for adding food point.

Attacked by enemy would lose your food point. Also, each step you walk would takes 1 food point.

Use arrow key to walk through the map.
